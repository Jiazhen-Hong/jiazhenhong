Jiazhen Hong is a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering at Rutgers University, supervised by Professor Laleh Najafizadeh. From 2024 to 2025, he was a Research Scientist Intern at EMOTIV, developing EEG foundation models on large-scale cloud infrastructure.

He holds a B.Sc. double degree from Jimei University and Chung Yuan Christian University (2016), an M.Sc. from Stevens Institute of Technology (2018), and conducted research at Harvard Medical School (2019–2020). His work has appeared in Nature Portfolio, IEEE TBME, NeurIPS, and ICDM. His research interests include brain-computer interfaces, EEG foundation models, LLM post-training, fine-tuning, and reasoning for assistive communication.
